Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 9, 2026

Introduction

GDD Tracker ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

Location Data

  • What we collect: Your device's location (latitude and longitude)
  • Why we collect it: To provide accurate local weather data and calculate Growing Degree Days specific to your lawn's location
  • How we use it: Location data is used solely to fetch weather information from our weather data provider
  • Storage: Location coordinates are stored locally on your device and in your iCloud account (if iCloud sync is enabled)
  • Sharing: We share your location coordinates with our weather data provider to retrieve forecast data

Usage Data

  • What we collect: App usage patterns, feature interactions, and crash reports (via Apple's built-in analytics)
  • Why we collect it: To improve app performance, fix bugs, and understand how users interact with the app
  • How we use it: Aggregated, anonymized data helps us make the app better
  • Control: You can disable analytics in your device Settings → Privacy & Security → Analytics & Improvements

Subscription Data

  • What we collect: Subscription status, purchase history, and transaction information
  • Why we collect it: To manage your subscription and provide access to premium features
  • How we use it: Processed by Apple through StoreKit; we receive only subscription status
  • Storage: Managed entirely by Apple; we do not store payment information

User-Created Content

  • What we collect: Lawn names, GDD settings, application records you create in the app
  • Storage: Stored locally on your device and optionally in your iCloud account
  • Sharing: Never shared with third parties

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide accurate Growing Degree Day calculations
  • Fetch local weather forecasts
  • Send notifications when treatment applications are due
  • Sync your data across your Apple devices (via iCloud)
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Provide customer support

Data Sharing & Third Parties

Weather Data Provider

We share your location coordinates with Apple Weather to retrieve weather forecasts. Please review their privacy policy at apple.com/legal/privacy.

Apple Services

  • iCloud: If enabled, your app data syncs via iCloud (managed by Apple)
  • StoreKit: Subscription management is handled by Apple
  • Push Notifications: Delivered through Apple Push Notification Service

We Do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Use your data for marketing purposes
  • Share data with any third parties except as listed above

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • All data stored locally uses iOS encryption
  • iCloud data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Location data is transmitted securely over HTTPS
  • No sensitive data is stored on our servers

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data stored in the app through the app interface
  • Delete: Remove your data by deleting the app from your device
  • Opt-out: Disable location services or notifications in Settings
  • Export: Your data is stored in standard formats within the app

Children's Privacy

GDD Tracker is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through:

  • An in-app notification
  • An update to this document with a new "Last Updated" date

Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Data Retention

  • Account deletion: Delete the app to remove all local data
  • iCloud data: Managed by Apple; delete from Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Storage
  • Subscription data: Retained by Apple for transaction history purposes
