Frequently Asked Questions

What's the typical response time?
We aim to respond to all support inquiries within 24–48 hours on business days. Feature requests are reviewed on a rolling basis and you'll receive a confirmation once submitted.
Which email should I use?
Use Support@GDD-Tracker.com for bugs, account issues, or general help. Use Features@GDD-Tracker.com to suggest new functionality or improvements you'd like to see.
How do I write a good feature request?
Describe the problem you're facing, the outcome you'd like, and any context about your workflow. The more detail you provide, the better we can evaluate and prioritize your request.
